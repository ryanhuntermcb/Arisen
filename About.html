Home

Documentation: 6 / 5 / 2004



Abstract: This website is for documentation of my findings and containment of unwanted materials. Some of the passages displayed were previously inscribed in letters or other medium and are only being relayed here. This website is meant to be a monument for the moment of discovery, and a monument to what it took to get here. If there ever was an "end of history", what is documented here is a rebirth of history.

Goals:
  1. To further contextualize our existence within the scientific supernatural
  2. To contain unwanted materials
  3. To serve as a maze in the event of said unwanted materials escaping
  4. To filter out the dedicated from the undedicated

Warning: There is risk in the pursuit of knowledge. I have come under a heavy burden due to what I have discovered. The few who I have shown my work, and thus its consequences, have become largely disillusioned with their lives. Their sense of meanings in many cases have been torn from them, especially in the cases that the viewing parties had typical religious views. I am constructing this website to require some amount of effort to get to the intersting parts. This is to help those who, understandably, have morbid curiosities about the nature of life and the thin layer beneath. Hopefully the puzzles will make most of you give up and live happy, blissful lives. This website is only for the determined who cannot bear to live not knowing.
